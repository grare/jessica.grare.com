About Me

My work is rooted in the fine arts: drawing and painting. I apply my knowledge of fine art and art histroy into my photography. My joy is to blur the lines between a painting and a photograph.

I cover a wide range of photography, including concerts, events, travel, and nature. My work has been exhibited in multiple art shows and galleries. With my concert work, I covered bands big and small, from Pittsburgh's treasured rock band Misaligned Mind to Grammy award winners such as A Great Big World and Bo Burnham.

When I'm not behind the camera, I work as a professional graphic designer and event planner. Otherwise I'm trying international foods, crafting, and taking care of my two boys.
