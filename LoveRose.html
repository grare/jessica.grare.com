The Pursuit of Happiness

Six years ago, I started my own event planning business. I was inspired by my experience as an event photographer, where I was around people who were happy to be in the moment. My business offers day-of coordination to full service planning. I apply my creative skills by designing invitations, signage, and wedding branding. Managing high budget events and vendor teams aided in my leadership and project coordination skills. When I had downtime, I photographed my events, using the images to promote my business.
